Let's take a closer look at the subjects you will be learning in this course:

Basic techniques jQuery

jQuery is like a magical wand that can transform your website from meh to wow. But before you can work your magic, you gotta know the basics. This means learning how to implement the script, which is like the foundation of all your jQuery wizardry.

Selectors

In the world of jQuery, selectors are like magic spells that help you find and manipulate elements on your website. Whether you're targeting a specific element or grabbing a whole bunch at once, selectors are a powerful tool in your web development arsenal.

Events

Events are like the heartbeat of your website. They keep things lively and responsive, allowing your users to interact with your site in all sorts of cool ways. With events, you can track down and handle all sorts of user interactions, from clicks to hovers to swipes.

Effects

Effects are the spice that makes your website pop. Whether you want a smooth fade-in effect or a snazzy slide-out menu, effects are the key to making your website stand out from the crowd.

jQuery UI

jQuery UI is like a treasure trove of UI elements that can take your website to the next level. From draggable elements to date pickers, jQuery UI has everything you need to create a polished, professional-looking website.

Animation with jQuery

With animation, you can take your website from boring to breathtaking. Whether it's a subtle transition or a full-blown animation, jQuery's animation tools give you the power to make your website truly come to life.

Select, add and configure plugins

Plugins are your little helpers that can make your life so much easier. With plugins, you can add all sorts of extra functionality to your website without having to write a ton of code yourself. It's like having a team of dedicated assistants working tirelessly behind the scenes.

JSON and AJAX

JSON and AJAX are like the ultimate tag team in web development. With JSON, you can easily transfer data between your website and external sources, while AJAX lets you do it without having to refresh the page. It's like having a direct line to the internet!

Define a custom plugin:

Want to take your jQuery skills to the next level? Creating your own custom plugin is where it's at! With a custom plugin, you can add some seriously unique functionality to your website that nobody else has. It's like being a mad scientist, but for web development.
